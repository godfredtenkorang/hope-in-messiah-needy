About Our Foundation

We Are In The Mission To Help The Helpless

Hope in messiah needy foundation is an independent social service functioning as a non-profit charity. Our charitable activities were motivated by the concern of high rate of poverty in some part of our country and the need to provide assistance.

01

Needy and Widows

We take special care for widows and the poor and needy. A person who is poor and widow are would be donated to help them.

02

Physically Challenged

We take care for those who are unable to perform an activity independently with some donations to be able to sustain them.

03

Church Facility

Building a church, hall or any other building within whicb a church program, event or activity is carried out.

Vision

The vision of Hope in Messiah Needy Foundation is to provide medical support to the Needy, portable water to destitute area, aid to the Needy society and help orphans and persons with physical disabilities.

Mission

The Hope in messiah needy foundation mission is to provide medical support to those in need, as well as portable water to communities in need, assistance to the Needy in society and assistance to those with physical disabilities and orphanages and showing them how Jesus Christ love Us.

Goals

1. Assisting those in need with medical care in depraved area
2. Providing communities in need with portable water
3. Providing assistance to those who are in need in society concerning their education
4. Assist those with physical disabilities as well as orphans
